# 多智能体协作模式图例说明 / Legend for Multi-Agent Collaboration

## 图例颜色说明 (Color Coding)
- **深蓝色 (Deep Blue)** 👔：代表 **Supervisor (路由调度)**，强调其全局控制与核心协调地位。  
  *Represents the Supervisor, emphasizing its global control and core coordination.*
- **青绿色 (Teal)** ⚙️：代表 **Workers (领域专家)**，象征微服务执行单元的专业性与高效运行。  
  *Represents Workers, symbolizing the professionalism and efficiency of microservice execution units.*
- **橙色 (Orange)** ➡️：代表 **协作流程 (Collaboration Flow)**，指代系统内数据与任务流转的动态过程。  
  *Represents Collaboration Flow, indicating the dynamic process of tasks routing.*
- **灰色 (Gray)** 💾：代表 **数据流与存储 (Data Flow & Storage)**，暗示底层基础设施的稳固与不可变性。  
  *Represents Data & Storage, implying the stability and immutability of the infrastructure.*
- **红色 (Red)** 🔒：代表 **安全与合规 (Security & Compliance)**，警示隐私保护与安全隔离的最高优先级。  
  *Represents Security, warning of the highest priority for privacy and isolation.*
- **紫色 (Purple)** 🚀：代表 **部署与运维 (Deployment & Ops)**，寓意 CI/CD 流水线与持续集成。  
  *Represents Ops, symbolizing CI/CD pipelines and continuous integration.*

## 图标说明 (Icons)
- **指挥 (Person)**：全局路由者 (Global Router)
- **齿轮 (Cog)**：执行专家 (Execution Expert)
- **箭头 (Arrow)**：流转路径 (Routing Path)
